1. Who We Are

Renli ("we", "us", "our") operates the Renli mobile application and website at renliai.app. We are the data controller responsible for your personal data.

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at privacy@renliai.app.

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the Renli app and website, wherever you are located. If you are in the UK or European Economic Area, you have additional rights described in Section 9.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

  • Providing the Service: Creating and managing your account, detecting deadlines from your calendar, sending notifications, and enabling app blocking functionality
  • Personalisation: Using your name, course, and situation to personalise messages and the in-app experience
  • AI-generated content: Your task name and deadline timing are sent to OpenAI's API to generate personalised notification messages. We do not send identifying personal information to OpenAI beyond what is necessary for message generation.
  • Work verification: Photos you submit are analysed by AI to confirm genuine task completion before apps are unlocked
  • Improving the Service: Aggregated, anonymised usage data helps us understand how to improve Renli
  • Communications: Sending you important service updates and, with your consent, product news
  • Legal compliance: Meeting our legal obligations and protecting our legal rights

We will never use your data for advertising purposes or sell it to third parties for any reason.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

If you are in the UK or EEA, we process your personal data on the following legal bases under UK GDPR and GDPR:

  • Contract performance: Processing necessary to provide the Service you have signed up for — account creation, task detection, notification delivery
  • Legitimate interests: Improving and maintaining the Service, fraud prevention, and security — where these interests are not overridden by your rights
  • Consent: Calendar access, notification permissions, and any optional data processing — you can withdraw consent at any time through your device settings or account settings
  • Legal obligation: Where we are required to process data to comply with applicable law

6. How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the Service. Specifically:

  • Account data: Retained for the lifetime of your account and deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Task and completion data: Retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion.
  • Work verification photos: Retained for 90 days after submission to allow for disputes, then permanently deleted
  • Anonymised usage data: May be retained indefinitely in aggregated, non-identifiable form
  • Legal records: Some data may be retained longer where required by applicable law

You can request deletion of your data at any time. See Section 9 for your rights.

7. How We Protect Your Data

We take data security seriously and implement appropriate technical and organisational measures including:

  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS/SSL
  • All data at rest is encrypted using AES-256 encryption
  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms — we never store plaintext passwords
  • Biometric credentials are stored in iOS Keychain with device-only access — they never leave your device
  • Row Level Security on our database ensures users can only access their own data
  • Access to production systems is restricted to authorised personnel only
  • Regular security reviews and updates

While we implement strong security measures, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. If you discover a security vulnerability please disclose it responsibly to security@renliai.app.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us at privacy@renliai.app and we will delete that data promptly.

Users between 13 and 18 must have parental or guardian consent to use the Service as described in our Terms of Service.

9. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@renliai.app or use the in-app account settings.

Right to Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Right to Rectification

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data — the "right to be forgotten".

Right to Restrict Processing

Request that we limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.

Right to Data Portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

Right to Object

Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing.

Right to Withdraw Consent

Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

Right to Complain

Lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

We will respond to all rights requests within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

Withdrawing Permissions

You can withdraw calendar access at any time through your iPhone Settings → Privacy → Calendars → Renli. You can withdraw notification permissions through Settings → Notifications → Renli. Withdrawing these permissions will limit the functionality of the Service but will not affect your account.

10. Cookies and Tracking

The Renli mobile app does not use cookies. Our website at renliai.app uses minimal essential cookies only:

  • Session cookies: To maintain your session on the website — deleted when you close your browser
  • Preference cookies: To remember any preferences you set on the website

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies on our website. We do not use cross-site tracking of any kind.
